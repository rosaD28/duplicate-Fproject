About I Am We

I Am We's goal is to create community and cohesion in Chicago's most underinvested neighborhoods by building bridges between youth with local community. Through programming, we hope to connect youth with local communities and give them the self-confidence needed to succeed academically and socially.

Programs

Chicago Voices

Chicago Voices is a program that provides an introduction to digital storytelling while providing youth with hands on experience in film-making.

Youth Speaks

Youth Speaks is a co-ed journalism program that introduces youth to the art of journalism, community engagement and the power of storytelling.

Unsung Heros

Unsung Heroes exposes youth to professional photographers and writers exposing them to interviewing, writing, photography and editing.
